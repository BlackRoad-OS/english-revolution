🚀 Phrasal Verbs - Direction = Meaning!

Same verb + different direction = completely different meaning!

LOOK + [direction]

👆 look UP = search/find
"I look 👆 the word in the dictionary" 🔍📖
👇 look DOWN = regard as inferior
"Don't look 👇 on poor people" 😔
👉 look INTO = investigate
"Police look 👉 the crime" 🔎🕵️
🛡️ look AFTER = take care of
"I look 🛡️ my baby sister" ❤️👶

GET + [direction]

👆 get UP = rise from bed
"I get 👆 at 7am" 🛌➡️🏃
🔚 get OVER = recover from
"I can't get 🔚 my ex" 😢➡️😊

🗣️ Pronunciation - See the Shape, Make the Sound!

No more confusing IPA symbols! Just see what to do with your mouth 👄 and tongue 👅

Vowel Sounds

😃 Long E (as in "see")
👄 BIG smile, teeth showing
🔊 "eee"
📝 see, tree, me, be
😮 O sound (as in "hot")
👄 Round open, like "oh!"
🔊 "ah" (American) or "oh" (British)
📝 hot, dog, shop, box

Tricky Consonants

😛 TH sound (as in "think" or "this")
👅 Tongue BETWEEN teeth!
🔊 "th" (think = no voice, this = with voice)
📝 think, this, mother, bath
🔄 R sound (American)
👅 Curl tongue back (don't touch roof!)
🔊 "rrr"
📝 red, car, very

Word Stress

Two patterns:
📢 = BIG (louder, longer, higher pitch)
🤫 = small (quieter, shorter, lower pitch)

Example: TA-ble (📢🤫), to-DAY (🤫📢)

Formality Levels

Power Words - Weak → Strong

❌ "did" → ✅ "achieved, accomplished, delivered"
❌ "made better" → ✅ "optimized, enhanced, improved"
❌ "helped with" → ✅ "spearheaded, led, drove"
❌ "worked on" → ✅ "developed, executed, implemented"

Meeting Phrases

🎯 Starting: "Thank you all for joining. Let's get started."
